## The angle

All four already run off-campus private-listing databases, and each one carries a disclaimer that the university does not assess the suitability of those listings, plus scam warnings to students. That is the wedge. Quni is not something they lack; it is the verified, fair version of a thing they already offer but explicitly cannot vouch for.

## Before sending

Confirm a real submission on quni.com.au/for-universities lands at hello@quni.com.au. These emails drive university staff straight to that form, so a silent failure burns first impressions with exactly the people you want.

## Notes on use

- Signature is set to Quinn (founder), since founder-to-office lands better cold. Swap if someone else manages replies.
- Greeting uses "[office / name]". Use the office name, or a named contact if their accommodation page lists one.
- Lead with Version 1 (Soft complement). Hold Version 2 (Name the gap) for a follow-up to anyone who does not respond.

## Email, Version 1: Soft complement (use first)

**Subject:** A verified housing option for your students

Hi [office / name],

I'm Quinn Lee, founder of Quni Living, a verified student-housing marketplace in Australia.

I know your team already points students toward off-campus listings. We built Quni to be the version you can actually vouch for. Every property comes from a verified private landlord, and the platform is built so international students can't be filtered out for being international: our matching tools never receive a student's nationality, gender, residency status, or date of birth, it's enforced in the code itself, and every decision is logged to a tamper-proof record. Students pay no fees.

For your office that means fewer housing queries to field, and less risk of students being scammed or quietly screened out of the private rental market.

A short overview is here: quni.com.au/for-universities

If it's useful, I'd be glad to give your students a verified, fair place to look, including a co-branded referral link for your accommodation and international pages. Happy to find 15 minutes whenever suits.

Thanks,
